What to expect in 2017

After the success of Merthyr Rising 2016 we're promising to bring you something bigger and better. Our aim is to make the Merthyr Rising Wales' leading town centre arts and ideas festivals.

More Music

This year will include a full weekend (3 days) of live music on the Unison Stage between Friday 26th May and Sunday 29th in Penderyn Square.

Thought Provoking Talks and Debates

This year we're introducing a new and imporived venue for what promises to be the most remarkable talks snce the festival begun.

Creative Workshops

Merthyr Rising will be working with the most talented creatives who'll be running workshops accesibel to anyone throught the entire week of the festival.

Much more for the family

Expect more for the family in 2017 especially for the kids. Entertainment will include rides, face painting, street perfomance and much more.

Wales' Best Street Food and Beer

We promise to bring the tastiest grub, beer and beverages in the area. We've a;ready secure the World's best Beef Burger by the famous Beefy Boys.

Radical Art, Theatre and Film

As expected we'll be showcasing the very best in local and radical arts, theatre and film. Stuff that'll entertain, entrigue and empower visitors of the festival.
